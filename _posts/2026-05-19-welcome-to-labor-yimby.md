## The Mission

The goal is more homes across Australia. The housing crisis is the defining economic issue of this generation, and it demands urgent action.

## Why YIMBY?

For too long, the conversation around housing has been dominated by those who say "no" to new development. The YIMBY movement is here to change that. The principles are:

- **Abundant housing**: Building enough homes to meet demand
- **Affordable communities**: Ensuring people can live near where they work
- **Sustainable growth**: Well-planned communities with good transport and infrastructure

## What We Stand For

Organising within the Labor Party to push for policies that will increase housing supply, reform planning systems, and make home ownership achievable for a new generation of Australians.

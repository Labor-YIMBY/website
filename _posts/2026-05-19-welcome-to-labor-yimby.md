## Our Mission

We're fighting for more homes across Australia. The housing crisis is the defining economic issue of our time, and it demands urgent action.

### Why YIMBY?

For too long, the conversation around housing has been dominated by those who say "no" to new development. We're here to change that. We believe in:

- **Abundant housing**: Building enough homes to meet demand
- **Affordable communities**: Ensuring people can live near where they work
- **Sustainable growth**: Well-planned communities with good transport and infrastructure

### What We're Doing

We're organising within the Labor Party to push for policies that will increase housing supply, reform planning systems, and make home ownership achievable for a new generation of Australians.
